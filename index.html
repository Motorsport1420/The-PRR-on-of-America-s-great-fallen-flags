      • Diesel Engines

      The PRR's diesel locomotives were classified first by the manufacturer, than the type of service, than the horsepower rounded to the hundreds, and finally, an extra variation letter.

      What the letters mean.

      Manufacturer Letters

      • A = Alco
      • B = Baldwin
      • E = EMD
      • F = Fairbanks Morse
      • G = GE
      • L = Lima

      Job Letters

      • F = Freight
      • H = Helper
      • P = Passenger
      • S = Switcher
      • PF = Dual Service(Passenger and Freight)
      • RS = Road Switcher
